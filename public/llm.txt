# Unfounded

Unfounded is a venture lab and ecosystem built from the coasts of Tamil Nadu, Chennai, shipping innovative Micro SaaS and deep-tech ventures to the global market. We follow an experiment-first philosophy, building and shipping daily to de-risk innovations for ourselves and our partners.

## Core Ventures

- **Ziggers**: Empowering the gig economy through localized labor orchestration.
- **LoopVerse**: A campus-focused ecosystem connecting student experiences.
- **Zachies**: A material innovation company focused on sustainable solutions.
- **DrinkQ**: Smart hydration and returnable packaging infrastructure.
- **DevFried**: A technical "deep fryer" that turns concept into code at high velocity.
- **LoopMemory**: An external brain/MCP integration for indexing personal and professional knowledge.
